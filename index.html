Understanding the Criminal Defense Process

From Arrest to Resolution

1

Initial Consultation & Case Evaluation

Free, confidential consultation where Scott Barton reviews charges, evidence, and your situation.

Timeline: Often same-day or within 24 hours

2

Investigation & Evidence Review

Scott Barton's team investigates the charges, reviews police reports, challenges improper evidence gathering.

Timeline: Weeks to months depending on complexity

3

Pre-Trial Motions & Negotiations

Filing motions to suppress illegal evidence, challenge improper procedures, or compel discovery. Often leads to charge reductions, dismissals, or better plea offers.

Timeline: Ongoing throughout case

4

Plea Negotiation or Trial Preparation

If prosecution's case is weak, negotiate favorable plea. If case is strong, prepare aggressively for trial.

Timeline: Varies

5

Trial (If Necessary)

Scott Barton defends you in court, presents defense witnesses, challenges prosecution evidence. Jury decides guilt or innocence.

Timeline: Days to weeks depending on case complexity

6

Sentencing or Appeal

If convicted, aggressive sentencing advocacy. If unjust conviction, explore appeals.

Timeline: Post-trial motions, appeal process can take months/years
